Open Source Camp

05 - 11 August 2017

About Open Source Camp

Conference

The Open Source gathering of the year in Romania, built with care by geeks for enthusiasts. Awesome technical speakers and talks and of course, loads of fun!

Workshops

Full-afternoons of workshops and hands-on experience, hacking and debugging. Join us in an epic battle to unleash the power of your favorite new technology!

Capture the Flag

If your plan is to play with the latest technologies here is the place. Test your knowledge and skills by solving problems and winning lots of prizes. Knowledge in various open source fields will be required to pass these challenges.

Networking

OSCamp is an event specially dedicated to all those interested in the open source perspective. We're waiting for you at the 2nd edition in Botoșani, where you can meet other like minded open source activists.

Latest news

OSCamp is coming soon! Check out the blog for all the details about our last edition and get ready for next summer, because is going to be awesome.
